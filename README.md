 A minimal and aesthetic habit tracker dashboard designed for focus and discipline. This is my personal system for tracking daily consistency across **Tech (MERN/Next.js)** and **Academics (PCM/JEE)**.

## Features

- **Dual-Track System:** Separate progress tracking for Tech and Academics.
- **Dynamic Heatmap:** A GitHub-style activity grid that provides a visual representation of your daily grind.
- **Fixed Sidebar UI:** Your progress stays on screen at all times—no more scrolling back and forth.
- **No-Bloat Design:** A clean, minimal, and dark-themed dashboard.

## Personal Use

This tool is optimized for my personal journey. I use it to stay on top of my goals and monitor my learning progress.

## Open for Everyone

If you are looking for a simple and fast tracker for your study or coding routine, feel free to use this. You can easily modify the tracks (Tech/PCM) to suit your own needs.
